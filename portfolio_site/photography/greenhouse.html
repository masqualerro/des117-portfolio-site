Greenhouse

Challenge Statement

I set out to capture an ethereal, dreamy neon ambience from the UV light at the greenhouse.

WHY

I chose to take photos at the greenhouse during the daytime and nighttime to display a contrast in the photoset. I also chose to include a photo of a person to establish an ambiguous narrative that the viewer could interpret. The UV light gave a green hue to dark colors that I think contributes to the surreal and dreamy atmosphere.
