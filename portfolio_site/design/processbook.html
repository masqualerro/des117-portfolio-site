Process Book

Challenge Statement

To conceptualize and design a “design intervention” public installation centered around the environment/nature, and outline my design process in a book.

WHY

After I took a trip to Mendocino, CA in September 2020, I became inspired by how well their natural environment was preserved and respected. A large motive behind my trip was to take photographs, and I found myself being incredibly engaged and appreciative of my surroundings while walking around with my camera. I hoped to bring that same appreciativeness to residents of my hometown of Davis, CA, and chose to center my project around natural environment preservation and mindfulness. I decided to design my installation as a public photo exhibit and interactive display meant to encourage mindfulness and engagement with local environments in Davis, CA. I extracted my color palette and general design aesthetics from the photos I took in Mendocino. Please read the process book for more ☺
