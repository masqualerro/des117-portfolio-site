Thundercat

Challenge Statement

Design a tour poster for the Japan tour of musician Thundercat, that is representative of his image, sound and branding.

WHY

I chose to use Cooper Black as the typeface for the poster because of its expressiveness and presence in Thundercat’s album art. I created a colorful repeated graphic of Thundercat to create a vibrant and exciting feeling, and chose a bold wine color for the background to catch the viewer’s eye. All of these choices are reminiscent of the fast paced, fun, and colorful feelings that Thundercat conveys in his music and personality.
